About Me

Once Upon My Musical Journey

Welcome to my little musical world! I'm Hailey from Singapore, and I'm so happy you're here. Ever since I can remember, music has been a magical part of my life. It follows me wherever I go. I love sharing music naturally, whether I'm performing on stage, in a concert hall, recording in a studio, learning during my lessons, or playing in beautiful places while travelling with my family. Every note I play becomes part of a precious memory, helping me discover new places, meet wonderful people, and grow not only as a young musician but also as a well-rounded person.

My musical journey began with the piano in 2023 and later blossomed into a love for the violin. I've been incredibly fortunate to learn from wonderful teachers who encourage me to believe in myself, embrace new challenges, and enjoy every step of the journey. They remind me that music is about so much more than playing the pieces. It's about telling stories, sharing joy, expressing feelings, and touching hearts.

When I'm not practising, you'll probably find me watching The Magic School Bus, Octonauts, Lucas the Spider, Hey Duggee, Trash Truck, and Bluey. I would like to recommend the animated movie Abominable because of its heartwarming story, beautiful music, and inspiring message about friendship, courage, and following your dreams.

Here's a little secret about me! I'm also a proud plushie collector! I love bringing home adorable plushies from our family adventures because each one reminds me of a special place and a beautiful memory. My favourite plushy is Yang Yang. Yang Yang always brings a smile to my face and is my cuddly little companion wherever I go. If we ever meet, I'd be absolutely delighted if you introduced me to another cute plushie to join our little family!

“Every melody is a little sprinkle of magic, every practice is an adventure, and every performance helps my dreams sparkle a little brighter.”

The Wonderful Teachers Who Believe in Me

My Piano Teacher – Teacher Clare

Teacher Clare is a lovely, caring, and dedicated piano teacher who is truly passionate about nurturing young children. Her patience, kindness, and encouraging nature make every lesson enjoyable and meaningful, especially for beginners like me.

When I first started learning piano in 2023, I had no musical background at all. I was simply a little girl excited and curious about attending my very first trial lesson. From the moment I met Teacher Clare, she made me feel comfortable and welcomed. She introduced me to the piano in a fun and engaging way, allowing me to explore the black and white keys and discover the joy of making music.

What I admire most about Teacher Clare is that she always gives her students the freedom and space to learn and grow at their own pace. She never rushes us or places unnecessary pressure on us. Instead, she creates a positive, supportive, and stress-free environment where learning feels natural and enjoyable.

One piece of advice she often shares with me is, “Enjoy the process, embrace every experience, and always play with your heart.” These simple yet meaningful words have stayed with me throughout my musical journey. Her positive teaching approach and unwavering encouragement have helped me develop not only my piano skills but also a genuine love for music.

My Violin Teacher – Teacher Diana

Teacher Diana is a professional, knowledgeable, and dedicated violin teacher who is devoted to inspiring and nurturing young children, teenagers, and adults to discover the joy and beauty of music. She believes that learning the violin is about much more than mastering techniques. It is about building confidence, nurturing creativity, developing resilience, and inspiring a lifelong love for music.

When I first joined Diana Violin Studio in 2025, Teacher Diana welcomed me with warmth and inspiration, making every lesson engaging, encouraging, and enjoyable. She patiently guided me through every challenge, helping me understand that learning the violin is a journey where every small step matters. Rather than focusing only on playing the pieces, she taught me how to listen attentively, express music with my heart, and enjoy the learning process.

What I admire most about Teacher Diana is that she truly understands that every child learns differently. She takes the time to understand each student's pace, personality, and strengths, adapting her teaching so every child feels supported and encouraged. Her lessons inspire curiosity, independent thinking, and the confidence to overcome challenges, allowing every student to grow into their own unique musician.

One of my favourite moments is playing duets with Teacher Diana. It always feels as though we are telling a beautiful musical story together. Playing alongside her has taught me to listen more attentively, play with greater confidence, and experience the joy of making music as a team. Every duet is filled with smiles, encouragement, laughter, and precious memories that inspire me to keep learning, growing, and always playing from the heart. Through her exceptional mentorship and unwavering belief in my potential, I have built confidence in my violin playing and discovered a profound, lifelong appreciation for music.
